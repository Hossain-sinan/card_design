Shalbon Bihar

Shalbon Bihar is an 8th-century ancient Buddhist monastery in Comilla, Bangladesh, part of the Mainamati-Lalmai archaeological complex. It was a large square structure with a central shrine, monastic cells, and subsidiary structures, serving as a center for learning and religious practice. It showcases rich history, with excavations revealing many artifacts from the 7th to 12th centuries.
